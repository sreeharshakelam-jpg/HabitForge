Virtue Forge — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Short version: Virtue Forge stores all your data on your iPhone. We do not have a server. We do not collect, transmit, or share any of your information. Ever.

1. Data We Collect

None. Virtue Forge does not collect any personal data from you. We do not ask for an email, phone number, name, or account to use the app.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The following information is stored locally on your iPhone only, using iOS on-device storage:

  • Your habits, streaks, and completion history
  • Your Discipline Score and Forge progression rank
  • Your preferences and notification settings
  • Any notes or reflections you add

This data never leaves your device. We cannot see it. No one at Virtue Forge has access to it.

3. No Server, No Backend

Virtue Forge operates entirely offline. We do not run any servers, databases, or cloud infrastructure that receives data from the app. The app works without any internet connection.

4. No Third-Party Services

Virtue Forge does not use:

  • Third-party analytics (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
  • Advertising networks or ad SDKs
  • Crash reporting services that transmit data off-device
  • Social media SDKs or tracking pixels
  • Any other third-party software development kits (SDKs)

5. Notifications

Virtue Forge uses iOS local notifications to remind you of scheduled habits. Notification scheduling happens entirely on your device. No notification content is sent to any server.

6. Children's Privacy

Virtue Forge is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not collect any information from any user, including children, because we do not collect any information at all.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete all your Virtue Forge data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the app from your iPhone, or
  • Using the "Reset Progress" option in the Forge (Profile) tab

Once deleted, your data is permanently gone — we have no backup because we never had a copy.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be announced in the app.
