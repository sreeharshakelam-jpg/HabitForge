FORGE Coach - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Overview

FORGE Coach ("the App") is a personal discipline app with an AI coach powered by Anthropic's Claude API. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data is handled and how.

Data Stored On Your Device

All habit data, streaks, points, preferences, profile information, and chat history with the AI Coach are stored locally on your iPhone using on-device storage. None of this data is transmitted to FORGE's servers — FORGE does not operate any backend servers.

Your Anthropic API Key (Bring Your Own Key)

To use the AI Coach feature, you must provide your own Anthropic API key, which you obtain directly from console.anthropic.com. Your API key is stored securely in the iOS Keychain on your device only. It is never sent to FORGE's servers.

AI Coach Data Transmission

When you interact with the AI Coach, the following data is sent directly from your device to Anthropic's API (api.anthropic.com) over an encrypted HTTPS connection:

  • Your typed message to the coach
  • A summary of your current habits, streak, discipline score, and today's progress (used as context so the coach can give relevant advice)
  • Recent conversation history with the coach

This data is processed by Anthropic under their own privacy policy: https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy. FORGE does not see, log, or store any of this data on its own servers.

Third-Party Services

FORGE Coach does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only third party involved is Anthropic (Claude API), and only when you actively use the AI Coach feature.

Notifications

FORGE Coach uses iOS local notifications to remind you of scheduled habits. Notification scheduling happens entirely on your device — no notification content leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

FORGE Coach is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the app or by using "Reset Progress" in the Profile settings. You can also remove your saved API key at any time from the Coach setup screen.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
