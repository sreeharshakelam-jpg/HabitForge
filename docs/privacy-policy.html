FORGE - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Overview

FORGE ("the App") is a habit tracking application. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

FORGE stores all your habit data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Data Storage

All habit entries, streaks, points, and preferences are stored locally on your iPhone using on-device storage. No data leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

FORGE does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

FORGE does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
